# 🎭 Agbalumo Vibe Check (Manual Verification)

This document is a mandatory manual checklist for human/reviewer verification of UX "vibes" and aesthetic standards. **All items must be checked before a feature is considered Done.**

## 🍽️ Food-First Experience
- [ ] **Discovery Velocity**: Can the user find a specific dish in < 60 seconds?
    - *Vibe: Snap-fast, non-fussy.*
- [ ] **Imagery Quality**: Do placeholder/generated images look appetizing and culturally accurate?
    - *Vibe: High-end food photography, not "stocky".*
- [ ] **Regional Clarity**: Are the country origins (e.g., Nigeria, Ghana) immediately visible and clear?

## 🏙️ Dallas Localization (MVP)
- [ ] **Neighborhood Accuracy**: Are the neighborhood names (e.g., Deep Ellum, Plano) correctly mapped and spelled?
- [ ] **Contact Vitals**: Is at least one valid contact method (WhatsApp/Email/Phone) functional and clickable?

## ✨ Aesthetic & Design
- [ ] **Brand Vibe (Sharp Editorial Earth)**: Does the UI feel premium, high-contrast, and editorial rather than "techy"?
- [ ] **Micro-animations**: Are hover states and transitions smooth (no jank)?
- [ ] **Glassmorphism**: Do overlays use valid semi-transparent blurs without sacrificing readability?
- [ ] **Zero-Radius**: Are all corners sharp (0px radius) unless explicitly excepted?

## 🛡️ Trust & Safety
- [ ] **Origin Validation**: Does every listing have a valid 'OwnerOrigin' country?
- [ ] **Data Gaps**: Are there any weird "Unknown" or "null" labels visible to the user?
