# User Persona: Ada, the Quality-Obsessed Consultant

| Attribute | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Name** | Ada |
| **Occupation** | Management Consultant (Frequent Traveler) |
| **Origin** | Nigeria |
| **Location** | Dallas, TX (Downtown) |
| **Core Goal** | To find the top 3 high-quality Nigerian food locations in any city in under 60 seconds. |

## Profile
Ada is a high-achieving professional who travels frequently for work. She is proud of her Nigerian heritage and finds comfort in authentic food and cultural services. She has little patience for mediocre experiences or clunky navigation.

## Pain Point Mapping

### 1. Quality Lottery
Ada knows how to cook; she has high expectations. Currently, trying a new place is a gamble. She needs a **Quality Signal** (consistent peer recommendations) that she can trust *before* she orders.

### 2. Time-to-Comfort
After a long travel day (e.g., Tuesday night in a new city), she needs immediate "home" comfort. The discovery process must be ultra-fast (< 60 seconds).

### 3. Community Isolation
She wants to see "people like her" ("Nigerians are everywhere"). The platform should signal not just food, but a safe/authentic community space.

## Secondary Needs: Cultural Services
Ada attends African weddings and wears traditional attire. Finding a **reliable tailor** is currently word-of-mouth and high-risk. She needs a trusted directory for these specialized services.

## Success Metrics for Ada
- **Discovery Time**: < 60 seconds from landing to quality food option.
- **Trust Factor**: Each listing must provide a clear quality signal (e.g., "Verified Authentic", "Top 3", or Peer Reviews).
- **Relevance**: No noise from irrelevant pillars (Jobs, Events, etc.) unless she explicitly seeks them.
